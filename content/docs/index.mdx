# Welcome to Growhouse

Growhouse is a visual canvas platform designed for growth teams to create, collaborate, and share content workflows.

## What is Growhouse?

Growhouse provides a flexible canvas where teams can:

- **Create visual content** - Design and organize content in a visual, intuitive way
- **Collaborate in real-time** - Work together with your team simultaneously
- **Share and publish** - Share canvases publicly or with specific team members

## Features

### Real-time Collaboration
Multiple users can work on the same canvas simultaneously with live cursor tracking and instant updates.

### Visual Canvas Editor
Drag-and-drop interface for creating and arranging content elements including text, images, videos, and embeds.

### Workspace Management
Organize your work into workspaces with role-based access control and team management.

### Public Sharing
Share your canvases publicly with a single click, perfect for portfolios and presentations.
