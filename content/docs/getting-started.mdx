# Getting Started

This guide will help you get started with Growhouse quickly.

## Create an Account

1. Visit [app.growhouse.co](https://app.growhouse.co)
2. Sign up with your email or Google account
3. You'll automatically get a trial workspace to explore

## Create Your First Canvas

Once you're logged in:

1. Click **"New Canvas"** in your workspace
2. Give your canvas a name
3. Start adding elements by clicking the **"+"** button or dragging from the toolbar

## Add Content

Growhouse supports multiple content types:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Text** | Rich text with formatting options |
| **Image** | Upload or paste images |
| **Video** | Embed videos from YouTube, Vimeo, etc. |
| **Embed** | Add content from other platforms |

## Collaborate

To invite team members:

1. Go to **Workspace Settings**
2. Click **"Invite Members"**
3. Enter their email address
4. Choose their role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer)
